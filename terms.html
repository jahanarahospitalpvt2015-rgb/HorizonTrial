2. Description of Services

Horizon is a clinical trial matching platform that connects patients and caregivers seeking clinical trials with Contract Research Organizations (CROs) seeking qualified participants, facilitated through physician engagement. The Platform provides:

  • For Patients and Caregivers: Health profile creation, AI-powered clinical trial matching, expression of interest in matched trials, management of contact requests from CROs, and notification services.
  • For Physicians: Clinical trial search and discovery tools, patient referral capabilities, saved trial management, and subscription-based access to advanced features.
  • For CROs: Trial listing management, access to anonymized matched candidate profiles, contact request tools, candidate pipeline management, and billing services.
  • For All Users: Account management, notification preferences, and consent management tools.

⚕️ Horizon does not provide medical advice, diagnoses, or treatment recommendations. The Platform is an informational and matching tool only.
